About Ethiopia

Ethiopia is a country in North Eastern Africa.
It is known as "the land of origins" because of its important archaeological and paleontological discoveries.
The earliest human ancestors were found in the Afar Depression in northeastern Ethiopia.
Ethiopia is the oldest independent country in Africa and one of the oldest in the world.
What are believed to be the oldest remains of a human ancestor ever found,
which have been dated as being some five million years old,
were discovered in the Awash Valley in Ethiopia.
This beats the discovery of “Lucy”, a 3.2 million year old skeleton,
who was unearthed in the same area in 1974.
